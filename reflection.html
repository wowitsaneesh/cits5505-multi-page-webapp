A critical reflection on the use of AI tools during this project.

How AI Was Used

AI tools were used to assist with planning the website structure, understanding lecture content, generating example code, and reviewing whether the implementation matched the project brief.

Example Prompts

  • Help me build the HTML structure of a four-page website using only lecture concepts.
  • Explain how to organise semantic HTML for a tutorial page.
  • Suggest a simple structure for an AI reflection log page.

What the AI Produced

The AI produced draft page structures, content suggestions, and examples of how lecture concepts could be applied to the project.

Corrections and Changes Made

Some output needed adjustment to ensure it stayed within the scope of the lecture material and matched assignment requirements exactly.

Limitations and Risks

AI output may be inaccurate, too advanced, or not fully aligned with the marking criteria. It must be checked carefully rather than copied without review.

Ethical Considerations

AI should be used as a support tool, not as a substitute for learning. It is important to acknowledge AI assistance honestly and critically evaluate its reliability.
